SkillSet Level Definition
Level 0: Huh?
I never heard of the topic and have no idea what it is.
Level 1: New, Awareness (What)
I am new to this topic. I have just attended a training or I have completed the self-study. I am able to recognize, remember and recall the terminologies or concepts but not necessarily be able to use or explain. Or it is merely data/material that is available from internet or knowledge bases. Able to explore the topic with reference to document or with guidance from Seniors
Level 2: Comprehension and Application (How)
I have a good grasp of the topic. I am able to explain related concepts and ideas. I can connect and apply this knowledge in a practical setting. I am able to associate the data/material with context/purpose and make it informative. Able to handle most support cases & technical build with design specifications. Able to deliver standard internal & client training
Level 3: Analysis (Why)
I've seen decent application / practices / approaches. I am able to provide an appraisal of the related concept or technique. I can make comparison with other concepts and see similarities and differences. I can well explain the link between business and the setup. I am able to understand what the information actually means and decides what's important. Aware of most of the traps and able to generalize best practices or advise the best approaches or application
Level 4: Synthesis (Wow)
I am able to organize and clearly present relevant information. I am prepared to lead a discussion of it. I am able to judge, defend or critique of choices or decisions by self or others. I am able to judge, decide what needs to be done with the knowledge to achieve certain goals, with experience and analysis. Able to construct, design and create own solutions to problems
